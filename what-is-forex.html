What Is the Forex Market?

The foreign exchange market, often called forex, is where people trade different currencies. Think of it like trading money when you travel to a foreign country.

What's unique about forex is that there's no one big building where trading happens. Instead, traders all around the world use computers to trade with each other.

Forex is open 24 hours a day, five and a half days a week. This means that when one trading center closes, another one opens. So, you can trade almost anytime you want, even at night!

Traders in big cities like London, New York, Tokyo, and more are busy buying and selling currencies. They're trying to make a profit by guessing which way currency prices will go.

It's like trying to predict whether the price of your favorite game will go up or down. If you're right, you make money!
